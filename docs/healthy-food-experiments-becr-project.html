5 Healthy Food Experiments (BECR Project)

I’m working with a BECR Center team that is piloting a few random experiments. The aim is to nudge people towards purchasing healthier snacks. They are working in partnership with Family Fare Convenience Stores to run the experiments and collect the data.

In exchange for a co-authorship, I am helping will all things data.
